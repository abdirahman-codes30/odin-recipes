Vanilla-iced-latte

Description

A vanilla iced latte is a refreshing coffee beverage that combines bold espresso, chilled milk, and sweet vanilla syrup over ice. It's smooth, lightly sweetened, and perfect for warm days or whenever you want a cool coffee treat. This simple recipe recreates the flavor of a café-style iced latte using just a few everyday ingredients.

Ingredients

  • shot (30 ml / 1 oz) freshly brewed espresso
  • 240 ml (1 cup) cold whole milk
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla syrup
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • Optional: Whipped cream for topping

Steps

  1. Brew a fresh shot of espresso and allow it to cool for a few minutes.
  2. Fill a tall glass with the ice cubes.
  3. Add the vanilla syrup to the glass.
  4. Pour the cold milk over the ice.
  5. Slowly pour the cooled espresso into the glass.
  6. Stir gently until the ingredients are well combined.
  7. Top with whipped cream if desired.
  8. Serve immediately with a straw or spoon.
